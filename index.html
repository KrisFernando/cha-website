Our Story

Cloud Heroes Africa

Founded on September 1st, 2021 by Samoa Shang and Rev Sister Jeannette to help youths find a productive way to grow their skills and experience in the emerging fields of Information Technology.

Our Mission

Provide free training, mentoring and job opportunities in the emerging fields of Information Technology.

Student Testimonials

I wish to say a big thank you to Cloud Heroes Africa for inspiring me to believe in myself and never giving up on my dreams. I started my studies with little to no knowledge in Tech and with a lot of difficulties and doubts, but thanks to dedicated teachers who never gave up on me, I passed my Cloud Practitioner exam. Now, I am studying for the Solution Architect Associate. I promise to give back the same positive energy to those coming behind me. God bless you all.

Clarise Kernyuy, Cameroon

I am happy to be a student of Cloud Heroes Africa. They have allowed me to enhance my skills and enriched my knowledge in Cloud computing. Two weeks after joining, I was advised to do the AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner exam, and I passed! I regularly participate in the weekly hands-on labs and exam preparations. In a few weeks, I will take the AWS Solutions Architect Associate exam. With effective teaching, mentoring, and guiding, my cloud skills are top-notch. Thank you, Cloud Heroes Africa.

Moses Balogun, Nigeria

Cloud Heroes Africa has helped me to learn everything I know about the cloud, especially AWS cloud. They have helped be to become an inquirer, searching for knowledge about AWS cloud services and how to use these services to build cloud solutions for businesses. Cloud Heroes Africa has opened my mind and encouraged me to take the risk of leaving my comfort zone as a high school teacher and counselor, and strive to change my profession to a cloud engineer. With Cloud Heroes Africa I have become a life long learner, as I keep developing myself professionally each day using the amazing resources it provides on different platforms like Discord, Whatsapp, LinkedIn, just to name a few. Thanks to cloud Heroes Africa, I am a certified cloud practitioner and, I am currently learning to become a solutions architect.

Sylvia Ngum, Cameroon
